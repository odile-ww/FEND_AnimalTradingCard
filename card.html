Red Panda

They use their bushy tail as a wrap-around blanket when cold. Also: Mozilla's Firefox browser is named after them!

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Length: 55 to 60 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 8 years (in the wild)
  • Habitat: Eastern Himalayas and China

Red pandas are arboreal, sedentary mammals with reddish-brown fur on the upper parts of their body and light fur on the face. Their diets consists mainly of bamboo leaves, but they also eat eggs and insects. Despite the name, they are not related with the giant pandas .
